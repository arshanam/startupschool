Boston Startup School is an immersive program which teaches you real-life skills to accelerate your career and make you an effective employee from day one. Choose a specialty track to master --Marketing, Software Development, Product & Design, or Sales & Business Development-- and gain access to the others through collaborative projects. Learn from experienced industry leaders on valuable topics like how startups get funding, how to build your professional network, and how to negotiate your compensation package. Get priority access to the hottest startup companies looking for top talent. Leave the program well-trained and part of an exclusive network that will kickstart your startup career.

CROSS TRACK :
Interdisciplinary work, where each team includes someone from every track, develops communication skills and allows students to build something substantial. The Sales & Business Development and Marketing tracks also team up to think analytically about the connection between marketing a product and building an effective sales force while the Product & Design and Software Development tracks work together to ideate and prototype products. During ‘Idea Jams’ and the culminating ‘Capstone Project’ teams work to solve Design Partners’ real-world challenges, something that individuals can point to as evidence of their newfound skills and experience.
